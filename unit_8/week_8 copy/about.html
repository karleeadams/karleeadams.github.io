About Us

Zion's Gateway Plaza is a proposed 143,484 square foot unique retail/office center positioned to stand at the crest of the highest hill on SR 9/State 1 Block from IHC insta-care to the west and the heart of downtown Hurricane just to the east.

Many new residential housing communities lie close to the proposed plaza. The project's prime location also presents an added benefit of un-obstructed and breathtaking views of Pine Valley Mountain, Zion National Park, and the nearby vibrant red and purple hued Vermillion Cliffs.

Location

Zion's Gateway Plaza has great visibility on the main artery SR 9/State Street located 1.5 miles between the newly proposed IHC hospital, the IHC InstaCare to the west, and downtown City of Hurricane to the east.
